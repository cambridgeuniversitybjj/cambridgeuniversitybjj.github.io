About

The Cambridge Jiu Jitsu Club is a student-run organization dedicated to the practice and promotion of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ). Our community includes Cambridge undergraduates, graduate students, and other Cambridge affiliates, creating a diverse and inclusive environment for martial arts training.

We welcome members of all skill levels, from absolute beginners to experienced practitioners. Our focus is on developing technical proficiency, physical fitness, and mental discipline through the art of BJJ.

What to Expect

Class Structure

  • Dynamic Warmups: Each class begins with relevant movements and drills
  • Technique Focus: Learn and practice new techniques with experienced partners
  • Live Training: Classes end with positional sparring and live rolling

Ground Rules

  • Wear athletic clothing: Follow IBJJF guidelines - close-fitting athletic wear, no zippers or jewelry
  • Maintain hygiene: Clean clothes, trimmed nails, shower after training
  • Practice safely: Control your movements, communicate with your partner, tap early and often

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience to join?

No experience necessary! We welcome complete beginners and provide all the guidance you need to get started.

What should I bring to my first class?

Just bring comfortable workout clothes and a water bottle. We can provide a gi for your first few classes.

How much does membership cost?

We offer flexible membership options. Contact us for current pricing and special offers for students.

Is there a trial period?

Yes! We offer a free trial week so you can experience our classes before committing to membership.
